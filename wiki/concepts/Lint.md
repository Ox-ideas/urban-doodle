Lint is the audit pass that checks the wiki for structural and semantic issues.

## Checks
- contradictions between pages
- stale claims superseded by newer sources
- orphan pages with no inbound links
- missing concept or entity pages for frequent terms
- broken or missing cross-references
- schema compliance and frontmatter quality

## Outcome
- a report of wiki health issues
- recommended fixes for the agent or human reviewer
- improved long-term reliability of the knowledge base
