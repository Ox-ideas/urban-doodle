Raw sources are the immutable original materials that feed the LLM Wiki. They include articles, transcripts, papers, code, data files, and other captured content.

## Purpose
- preserve the original source material unchanged
- provide a stable reference for citations and traceability
- allow the wiki layer to evolve without modifying raw inputs

## Characteristics
- stored under `raw/sources/`
- may include local assets in `raw/assets/`
- never modified by the ingest process
- always cited from wiki pages
