# Concept: Atomic Notes

Atomic notes are small, focused pieces of information (typically 50–300 tokens) designed for efficient retrieval and reasoning by AI. This approach contrasts with traditional, lengthy notes or documents, which are less efficient for both human and AI consumption.

- **Granularity**: Each note covers a single idea, fact, or decision.
- **Efficiency**: Reduces token usage and improves AI performance.
- **Composability**: Atomic notes can be linked and recombined to answer complex queries.

Atomic notes are a core pillar of the "Infinite Brain" and modern knowledge graph systems.
