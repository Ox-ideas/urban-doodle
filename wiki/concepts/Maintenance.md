Maintenance is the ongoing process that keeps the LLM Wiki consistent, accurate, and useful.

## Components
- rebuild the catalog and index
- validate that new changes follow schema rules
- check for missing pages and broken references
- run query verification to ensure routing and retrieval work

## Goal
- ensure the wiki remains a reliable knowledge source over time
- catch structural issues before they compound
- support continuous improvement of the vault
