Infinite Brain is an AI-centric knowledge architecture that replaces generic PKM structures with more specific note types and richer relationship semantics.

## Principles
- Use atomic notes to keep content small and focused.
- Prefer page lengths of roughly 50–300 words when possible.
- Organize information into explicit edge types rather than untyped links.
- Let AI drive the structure, while still preserving human interpretability.

## Benefits
- reduces token waste during retrieval
- makes relationships easier to reason over
- improves the quality of AI-guided navigation
- supports faster, more reliable answers from the wiki
