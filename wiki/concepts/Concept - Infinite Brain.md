# Concept: Infinite Brain

A knowledge management system designed for AI-native workflows, introduced as an evolution beyond the PARA (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives) and "Second Brain" methods. The Infinite Brain system emphasizes:

- **Atomic Notes**: Small, focused notes (50–300 tokens) for efficient AI retrieval.
- **Typed Nodes**: 16 node types (pillars, decisions, concepts, questions, playbooks, etc.) to provide structure and clarity.
- **Complex Edge Types**: Richer relationships (supports, contradicts, depends on, derived from, part of, authored, etc.) for explicit knowledge linking.
- **Token Efficiency**: Designed to minimize token usage and maximize AI comprehension.

The Infinite Brain is intended to be both human-usable and highly optimized for AI agents, enabling persistent, evolving, and richly linked knowledge graphs.
