# Concept: Knowledge Graph

A structured network of interconnected notes or data points, designed to represent relationships and dependencies between concepts, entities, decisions, and facts. In the context of AI-native knowledge management (as described by [[AI Impact]]), knowledge graphs:

- Enable high-quality, context-aware AI by providing explicit, typed links between information.
- Support complex edge types (supports, contradicts, depends on, derived from, part of, authored, etc.) for richer reasoning.
- Are more efficient for AI retrieval and synthesis than traditional, human-centric note-taking systems.
