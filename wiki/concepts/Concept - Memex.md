# Memex

A hypothetical personal knowledge store described by **Vannevar Bush** in his 1945 article *"As We May Think"*. 

The Memex (Memory + Index) was envisioned as a device for individuals to store books and communications, search them rapidly, and create **associative trails** between documents. The [[LLM Wiki]] is seen as a modern realization of this vision, with the LLM solving the "maintenance problem" that hindered previous attempts at building personal wikis.
