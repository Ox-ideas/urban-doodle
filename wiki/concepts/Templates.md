Templates capture the standard structure for different wiki page types.

## Purpose
- enforce repeatable formatting for sources, concepts, entities, comparisons, and logs
- help agents create new pages consistently
- reduce manual editing and improve quality control

## Usage
- store templates in a dedicated `templates/` folder
- include the required frontmatter fields and recommended section layout
- update templates to improve future page creation
