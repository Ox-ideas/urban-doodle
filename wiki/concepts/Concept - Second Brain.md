# Concept: Second Brain

A personal knowledge management (PKM) philosophy popularized by [[Tiago Forte]], centered on the PARA method. The "Second Brain" approach focuses on organizing information for human retrieval and productivity. In the context of AI-native knowledge management, it is critiqued for being too human-centric and lacking the explicit structure needed for advanced AI reasoning.

- **PARA Method**: Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives.
- **Human-centric**: Designed for human usability, not AI.
- **Contrast with Infinite Brain**: The Infinite Brain system proposes more granular, typed, and AI-optimized notes and relationships.
