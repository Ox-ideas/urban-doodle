# Andrej Karpathy

Andrej Karpathy is a prominent AI researcher, former AI lead at Tesla, and co-founder of OpenAI. He is known for his work on computer vision, deep learning, and for popularizing the concept of [[LLM Wiki]] through an "Idea File" on GitHub.
