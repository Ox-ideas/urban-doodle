# Comparison: Infinite Brain vs Second Brain (PARA)

| Feature                | Infinite Brain (AI Impact)         | Second Brain (PARA, Tiago Forte) |
|------------------------|------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Primary Audience       | AI agents & humans                 | Humans                            |
| Note Structure         | Atomic, typed (16+ node types)     | Four broad categories (PARA)      |
| Edge Types             | Rich, explicit (supports, contradicts, etc.) | Simple links                    |
| Token Efficiency       | Highly optimized for AI retrieval  | Less efficient, large notes       |
| Flexibility            | Highly granular, composable        | Coarse, less granular             |
| Relationship Clarity   | Explicit, machine-readable         | Implicit, human-centric           |
| Example Tools          | Obsidian (AI-native setup)         | Obsidian, Notion, Evernote        |
| Critique               | May be complex for humans          | Not optimized for AI              |

## Summary

The Infinite Brain system is designed for AI-native workflows, emphasizing granularity, explicit relationships, and token efficiency. The Second Brain (PARA) method is optimized for human usability but lacks the structure needed for advanced AI reasoning and retrieval.
